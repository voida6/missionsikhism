Sikhs today live on every continent, carrying gurdwaras, langar and festival to cities far from the Punjab. The diaspora — shaped in part by the upheaval of [Partition](/events/partition-of-punjab) and by later migration — has made Sikh culture a genuinely global presence while keeping its roots in the land of the Gurus.
