## About

Banda Singh Bahadur — Military commander, first Sikh sovereign. The commander who, on Guru Gobind Singh's authority, established the first sovereign Sikh rule in the Punjab, defeated the Mughal governor of Sirhind in 1710, and was executed in Delhi in 1716.
