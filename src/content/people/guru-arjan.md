## About

Guru Arjan Dev Ji — Fifth Sikh Guru, first Sikh martyr. Who compiled the Adi Granth and installed it in the Harmandir Sahib in 1604, completed the Golden Temple, and became the first Sikh martyr when he was executed under the Mughal emperor Jahangir in 1606.
