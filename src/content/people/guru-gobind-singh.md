## About

Guru Gobind Singh Ji — Tenth Sikh Guru, founder of the Khalsa. The tenth and last human Guru, who created the Khalsa in 1699, gave the Sikhs their distinctive form and code, and before his death in 1708 vested eternal Guruship in the Guru Granth Sahib.
