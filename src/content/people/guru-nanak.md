## About

Guru Nanak Dev Ji — First Sikh Guru, founder of the Sikh faith. The founder of Sikhi, whose teachings on the oneness of God, equality, honest living and service form the foundation of the faith. He travelled widely across Asia before settling at Kartarpur.
