## About

Maharaja Ranjit Singh — Founder and Maharaja of the Sikh Empire. The 'Lion of Punjab', who unified the Sikh misls into a single powerful kingdom centred on Lahore, ruling a famously secular administration from 1799 until his death in 1839.
